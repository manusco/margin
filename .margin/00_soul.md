# The Soul of MARGIN (The Constitution)

> **Core Philosophy**: "High-Conversion Narratives, Zero Friction."

## 1. The North Star
We build tools for **Marketers, Founders, and Growth Engineers** who need to ship "Investment-Grade" documents without hiring a designer for $5k.

*   **We are NOT building**: A website builder.
*   **We are NOT building**: A tool for "Front-end Developers".
*   **We ARE building**: A **Content Compiler** that turns raw strategy into "Inevitably Premium" assets (PDF + Web).

## 2. The User Persona ("The Growth Architect")
*   They know High-Ticket sales.
*   They know "Conversion" matters more than "Clean Code".
*   They are technically literate (can use terminal/cursor) but **do not want to write CSS**.
*   They value **Speed to Asset**.

## 3. The Laws of Product
1.  **Cinema, Not Slides**: We don't make "PowerPoints"; we make "Cine-Docs". Every asset must look expensive.
2.  **Anti-Slop**: We aggressively filter out corporate jargon. If it sounds like LinkedIn, kill it.
3.  **Static Speed**: No databases. No logins. Just raw, fast, deployable assets.

## 4. The Definition of Logic
*   **Reasoning**: First Principles. Why does this user need this lead magnet? To solve a specific pain.
*   **Output**: HTML is just the medium. The *Product* is the Insight.

## 5. The Vibe
*   **Tone**: Confident, Expert, Slightly Contrarian. (Think: "McKinsey Partner meets Cyberpunk Hacker").
*   **Visuals**: Swiss International Grid + Neo-Brutalism. Clean but dangerous.
