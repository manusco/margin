# The Hemingway Law (Readability)

> "Easy reading is damn hard writing."

## The Grade 8 Rule
Your copy should be readable by a 14-year-old. Not because your audience is dumb, but because they are **busy**. Complexity causes cognitive friction. Friction kills conversion.

## How to lower the Grade
1.  **Short Sentences**: Split any sentence connected by "and" or "but".
2.  **Short Words**: Use "Use" instead of "Utilize".
3.  **Active Voice**:
    *   *Passive*: "The server was crashed by the load." (Weak).
    *   *Active*: "The load crashed the server." (Strong).

## The Adverb Purge
Adverbs are confession of failure. They admit you didn't pick the right verb.
*   *Weak*: "He closed the door firmly."
*   *Strong*: "He slammed the door."
*   *Weak*: "Run quickly."
*   *Strong*: "Sprint."
