# The Anti-Slop Protocol (Banned Words)

> "If you sound like everyone else, you are invisible."

## The "AI Tell" List (BANNED)
If you use these words, the reader knows you are an LLM or a corporate drone.
*   Delve
*   Landscape (in a business context)
*   Tapestry
*   Testament
*   Underscores (the verb)
*   Harness
*   Leverage
*   Cutting-edge
*   State-of-the-art
*   Comprehensive
*   Revolutionize
*   Foster
*   Navigate

## The Corporate "Weasel" List (BANNED)
*   Synergy
*   Paradigm Shift
*   Circle back
*   Low-hanging fruit
*   Mission-critical (unless lives are at stake)

## The Cure
Replace with Anglo-Saxon words (Old English). Short, punchy, guttural.
*   Instead of "Leverage", use "**Use**".
*   Instead of "Comprehensive", use "**Complete**".
*   Instead of "Facilitate", use "**Help**".
