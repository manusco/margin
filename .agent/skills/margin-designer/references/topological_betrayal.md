# Topological Betrayal (Layout Theory)

> "The eye ignores patterns. It focuses on the break in the pattern."

## The Theory
Human brains are prediction engines. When they see a grid (bootstrappy 3-column layout), they go into "Auto-Pilot" (Scanning mode). They stop reading.
To force attention (Reading mode), you must **Betray the Topology**.

## Techniques
1.  **The Bleed**: Let an image run off the edge of the container. It implies a world beyond the frame.
2.  **The Overlap**: Text on top of Image. Image on top of Text. It creates depth (Z-Axis).
3.  **The Asymmetry**: If the left column is 50%, do NOT make the right column 50%. Make it 30% and leave 20% void.
4.  **The Micro-Rotation**: Rotate an element 2deg. It creates palpable tension.

## The Rule
Every slide must have **one** element that feels "out of place" or "dangerous".
