# Gestalt Principles in Data (Grouping)

> "The whole is other than the sum of the parts."

## 1. Proximity
Objects close to each other are perceived as a group.
*   *Application*: Put the Label **inside** or **immediately next to** the bar. Not in a legend far away.

## 2. Similarity
Objects that look alike are perceived as related.
*   *Application*: If "Revenue" is Green in Chart A, it MUST be Green in Chart B. Do not change colors for "variety".

## 3. Enclosure
Objects enclosed by a boundary are grouped.
*   *Application*: Use a subtle glassmorphism panel to group "Q4 Metrics" distinct from "Q1 Metrics".

## 4. Continuity
The eye follows lines.
*   *Application*: Use smooth curves (splines) for trends to imply organic growth. Use sharp angles for volatile data.
