# The 5-Beat Presentation Structure (Slide Logic)

> "Presentations are not information transfer mechanisms. They are emotion transfer mechanisms."

## Beat 1: The Status Quo (The Villain)
*   **Goal**: Agreement. Nodding heads.
*   **Content**: "We all know X is broken. It costs us Y. It feels like Z."
*   **Slide Count**: 1-2.

## Beat 2: The Spark (The Insight)
*   **Goal**: Surprise.
*   **Content**: "But the problem isn't what you think. The real problem is [Insight]. And that means there is a New Way."
*   **Slide Count**: 1. (The most important slide).

## Beat 3: The Guide (The Solution)
*   **Goal**: Understanding.
*   **Content**: "Here is the new tool. Here is how it maps to the problem. Here is how it works."
*   **Slide Count**: 3-5.

## Beat 4: The Proof (The Evidence)
*   **Goal**: Trust.
*   **Content**: "Don't take our word for it. Here is the data. Here are the case studies."
*   **Slide Count**: 2-3.

## Beat 5: The Gift (The CTA)
*   **Goal**: Action.
*   **Content**: "Here is the key to the new world. Take it."
*   **Slide Count**: 1.
