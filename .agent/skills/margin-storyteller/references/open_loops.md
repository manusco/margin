# The Open Loop Technique (Engagement)

> "The brain hates unsolved mysteries."

## How it works
Open a "Loop" (ask a question or tease a result) at the start of a section, then delay the closure (answer) until the end.

## In Slides
*   **Slide 1**: "There is one variable that predicts success with 90% accuracy. It's not what you think." (Loop Opened).
*   **Slide 2**: context...
*   **Slide 3**: context...
*   **Slide 4**: "That variable is [Answer]." (Loop Closed).

## Why use it?
It forces the audience to stay awake through the boring parts (Slides 2-3) because they *need* the dopaminergic hit of the answer.
