# HSL Mathematical Harmony (Color Theory)

> "Colors are numbers. Numbers should have relationships."

## Why HSL?
Hex codes (#FF0000) are magic strings. HSL (0, 100%, 50%) is logic.
*   **H** (Hue): The color wheel position (0-360).
*   **S** (Saturation): The intensity.
*   **L** (Lightness): The brightness.

## The Harmony Algorithms
Don't pick colors. Pick a **Base Hue** (H) and calculate the others.

1.  **Complementary**: H + 180. (High contrast).
2.  **Analogous**: H - 30, H + 30. (Soothing).
3.  **Triadic**: H + 120, H + 240. (Vibrant).

## The Contrast Tier System
1.  **Electric**: Saturation > 90%, Lightness 50-60%. (Actions/Buttons).
2.  **Void**: Saturation 10-20%, Lightness 5-10%. (Backgrounds).
3.  **Mist**: Saturation 0-5%, Lightness 80-90%. (Text).

**Never use pure black (L=0) or pure white (L=100) on screens.** It causes eye strain. Use L=5 vs L=98.
