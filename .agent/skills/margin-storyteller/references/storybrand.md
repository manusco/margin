# The StoryBrand Framework (Narrative Arc)

> "The customer is the Hero, not your brand."

## The SB7 Framework

1.  **A Character (The Hero/User)**...
    *   Goal: They want to [Achieve X].
2.  **Has a Problem (The Villain)**...
    *   External: The physical problem (e.g., "Software is slow").
    *   Internal: How it makes them feel (e.g., "I feel incompetent").
    *   Philosophical: Why it's just wrong (e.g., "It shouldn't be this hard").
3.  **And Meets a Guide (Us)**...
    *   Empathy: "We know how it feels."
    *   Authority: "We have solved this before."
4.  **Who Gives Them a Plan**...
    *   Process Plan: "Step 1, Step 2, Step 3."
    *   Agreement Plan: "We promise X."
5.  **And Calls Them to Action**...
    *   Direct CTA: "Buy Now."
    *   Transitional CTA: "Read the PDF."
6.  **That Helps Them Avoid Failure**...
    *   "If you don't buy, you will lose money/status."
7.  **And Ends in a Success**.
    *   "You will be the hero of the office."
