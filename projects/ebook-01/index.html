Manifesto

Why build with
code?

"Traditional design tools (InDesign, Canva) are silos. They separate the content from the logic. MARGIN merges them into a single, declarative truth."

By using HTML and Tailwind CSS, we leverage the most powerful layout engine in history: The Modern Browser. MARGIN adds the guardrails needed to make that engine respect the physical boundaries of paper.

Atomic Design

Every element is a component. Every layout is a composition. We don't "draw" pages; we build them using a systematic approach.

AI Synergy

Our flat architecture is specifically designed for LLMs to read, refactor, and 'vibe' with your layouts in real-time.

1. The Document Wrapper

Everything starts with .document. This is your canvas. It enforces global rules and resets that Paged.js uses to slice your content.

2. The Page Section

Each section.page is a physical page. Paged.js will automatically handle page breaks if your content overflows, but for pixel-perfection, we recommend one section per page.

3. The Safety Zone

The dotted blue line you see in your browser? That's your guardian. Keep all critical text inside it to ensure zero clipping during PDF export.
